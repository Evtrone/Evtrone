About EVTRONE

Pioneering a sustainable future for India's electric mobility.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and innovative EV charging brand, empowering an accessible and sustainable electric mobility ecosystem for everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

To design, manufacture, and deliver high-quality, reliable, and smart EV charging solutions across India, backed by unparalleled customer support and a commitment to green technology.

The EVTRONE Story: Fueling India's EV Revolution

At EVTRONE, we believe in the transformative power of electric mobility. Founded with a vision to accelerate India's transition to a sustainable transportation future, we embarked on a journey to provide cutting-edge EV charging infrastructure that is both accessible and reliable.

Our roots are deeply embedded in Indian innovation. We combine local engineering prowess with global technological advancements to create products tailored for the diverse needs of the Indian market. From the bustling streets of metropolitan cities to the quiet highways, our solutions are designed to keep India moving.

We started small, with a dedicated team of engineers and visionaries passionate about clean energy. Today, EVTRONE stands as a testament to perseverance, quality, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Every charger we produce, every installation we complete, and every customer we serve is a step towards a greener, more electrified India.

Our commitment extends beyond just manufacturing. We are actively involved in R&D, exploring new frontiers in smart charging, solar integration, and battery management to ensure we remain at the forefront of the industry. We are not just selling chargers; we are building a network, a community, and a legacy of sustainable progress.
