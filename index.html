About EVTRONE

Story of Innovation

Founded on the principles of sustainable tomorrow, EVTRONE is driven by a vision to power India's electric vehicle revolution. We are a pioneering Indian company committed to designing, manufacturing, and installing advanced EV charging solutions.

Vision for Sustainable Mobility

Our vision is to build a robust, accessible, and smart EV charging infrastructure across India, enabling a cleaner, greener future for generations to come.

Pioneering Indian Engineering: With a strong focus on R&D, we pride ourselves on leveraging cutting-edge technology and superior Indian engineering to deliver IS & BIS certified chargers that are safe, reliable, and highly efficient. Our commitment to quality and safety is unwavering.
